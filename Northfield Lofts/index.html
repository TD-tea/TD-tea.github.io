Modern Living, Thoughtfully Designed

Experience a new standard of upscale living at Northfield Lofts, a 138-unit luxury apartment community arriving in Northfield, MN in 2027. Thoughtfully crafted for comfort and convenience, Northfield Lofts blends modern style with resort-level amenities—all just moments from the city's most popular shops and essentials.

Resort-Level Amenities

At Northfield Lofts, you're surrounded by spaces that inspire both relaxation and productivity.

  • Tiered outdoor patio for lounging, grilling, and gathering
  • State-of-the-art fitness center
  • Sauna + hot tub
  • Resident lounge & co-working areas
  • Pet-friendly grounds and walking paths
  • Heated parking with EV charging

Unbeatable Convenience

Live steps from the best of Northfield's retail and everyday essentials. With Target, Cub Foods, Aldi, and other major retailers just minutes away, errands become quick, easy, and stress-free.

Academic Community

Perfectly positioned for students, faculty, and staff of Carleton College and St. Olaf College. Both campuses are just a short drive or bike ride away, making it easy to attend classes and enjoy college life.
